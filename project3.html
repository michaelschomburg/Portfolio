Unicef Story //December 2016

Description

UNICEF Story is a drawing app for children. The drawing app is part of a bigger redesign of the UNICEF fundraising campaign stands in the Netherlands. UNICEF story is an engaging activity for children while their parents get informed about current UNICEF efforts. However the children are not just simply drawing something to be entertained. The concept is that those drawings get sent home to them and eventually will cover the aid packages. This way they will sent a personal note on an aid package which will eventually help a child in need. From children for children. This project was done in supervision of Het Proces design studio. The App does not exist publicly since it was only used for fundraising purposes.

Role

This project was done in a one week sprint. In a team of two my role was mainly to design the user story of the app as well as designing the Iconography.
