Coding is way too often too inaccessible for people with a different career path or students of a different background. Especially during my studies I wished that I would know how to code. This workshop was initialised in corporation with The Hague University of applied sciences and the department of Industrial Design. The workshop aimed to break the barrier for design students to the world of code and teach them basic programming in the languages of the web, HTML, CSS and Javascript. The workshop consisted of one lecture and four workshop sessions. The lecture was an introduction into the matter with different topics such as "what is programming?", the different touch points for designer with coding as well as the more conceptional topic of "computational thinking"
