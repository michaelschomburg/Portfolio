What to DO //April 2018

Description

I love google chrome extensions for their ingle purpose functionality and that there are extensions for anything. However, whenever I looked for a to-do list extension or a note extension I could usually only find one or the other. Often they had too many functions or where simply just ugly. What to DO aims to close this gap and provide a simple functionality and a clean design.
The project can be found here: What to DO

Role

This project was completely self initiated and I was responsible for everything from the idea to the execution
