I grew up in the industrial influenced area of Wolfsburg but spent the last years among different cultures and lifestyles in Manchester as well as Den Haag. I have a passion not only for technology and design but also for good records, football and a good cup of tea.

As a designer and aspiring Front-end Developer I am driven by the prospect that physical and digital products are equals, and when combined, have a significant influence on the human behaviour. Being a digital native that has experienced the development of the social media era, I see a need for flawlessly implemented products, that keep up with our ever changing digital environment. I finished my studys in Industrial Design in the Netherlands and put my focus on UX/UI Design. I am currently working on connecting the design world and the tech world through supplementing my design skills with front-end development. I do this through selfinitiated projects which you can find throughout this website as well as at the online school "TeamTreehouse".

If you want to get in touch with me, I would love to hear from you. So lets have a chat!
